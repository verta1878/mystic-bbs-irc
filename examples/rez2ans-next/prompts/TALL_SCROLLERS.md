# Tall Scrollers and Announcements

Use this for vertical BBS announcements, event art, advertisements, or long ANSI scrollers.

```text
vertical retro-computing poster of [SUBJECT], tall portrait composition,
primary subject in the upper third, strong silhouette, thick outlines, large
simple shapes, limited 8 to 12 color palette, high contrast, flat cel shading,
simple background with clear empty areas between elements, space reserved for
hand-added ANSI lettering, 1980s BBS scene art, no text, no watermark, no
signature
```

Keep important faces, symbols, and objects away from the very edges. Generate the artwork without words, then add readable lettering after conversion in an ANSI editor.

Use the `tall-scroller.ini` profile as a starting point.
